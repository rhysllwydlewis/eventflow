Your Data Rights

Last updated: December 2024

At EventFlow, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have important rights regarding your personal information.

This page provides a user-friendly explanation of your rights and how to exercise them. For complete details, please refer to our Privacy Notice.

How to Make a Request

To exercise any of your data rights:

  1. Email us: Send your request to admin@event-flow.co.uk
  2. Include:
    • Your full name
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Clear description of your request
    • Any relevant account details to help us identify you
  3. Verification: We may ask for additional information to verify your identity before processing your request
  4. Response: We will respond within 30 days (or notify you if we need more time)

Response Times

We are committed to responding to your requests promptly:

  • Standard response: Within 30 days of receiving your request
  • Complex requests: We may extend this by up to 60 additional days if necessary. We will notify you within the first 30 days if an extension is needed.
  • Free of charge: We will not charge a fee for most requests
  • Excessive requests: If your request is clearly unfounded or excessive, we may charge a reasonable fee or refuse the request

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete my account?

Email admin@event-flow.co.uk with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will confirm your identity and permanently delete your account within 30 days. Note that deleted data enters a 30-day recovery period before permanent deletion.

Q: Can I update my profile information myself?

Yes! Most profile information can be updated directly in your Settings page. Changes take effect immediately.

Q: What format will my data export be in?

Data exports are provided in JSON format (a standard machine-readable format). We can also provide CSV format for certain data types upon request.

Q: How long does it take to process a data request?

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days. Complex requests may take longer, but we will notify you if we need additional time.

Q: Is there a fee for data requests?

No, we do not charge a fee for reasonable data requests. However, we reserve the right to charge a reasonable fee or refuse clearly unfounded or excessive requests.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with your response?

If you're not satisfied with how we've handled your request, you can lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection supervisory authority. Visit ico.org.uk or call 0303 123 1113.
