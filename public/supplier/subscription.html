Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free trial?

During your free trial, you'll have full access to all features of your chosen plan. You won't be charged until the trial period ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you change plans, we'll calculate a pro-rated amount based on your current billing cycle.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from this page or your dashboard. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Stripe, which supports all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and more. Stripe provides secure, fast checkout with industry-leading security.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, absolutely. All payments are processed securely through Stripe, which uses industry-standard encryption and PCI-DSS Level 1 certification. We never store your payment information on our servers - all card data is handled by Stripe.
