What is EventFlow?

EventFlow is a simple planning tool that helps you organise events. You can discover venues and suppliers, build a shortlist, keep notes, and message suppliers — all in one place.

Is EventFlow free for customers?

Yes. Creating an account and using the planning tools is free for customers. In the future we may introduce optional “pro” features, but the core planning experience will remain free.

Do I need an account to get started?

You can browse suppliers without an account. To save a plan, add notes, or send messages to suppliers, you’ll need to sign up for a free EventFlow account.

How do enquiries and messages work?

When you start a conversation with a supplier, EventFlow creates a thread between the two of you. You can view and reply to messages from your dashboard, and depending on your settings we may also send you email notifications when there is a new message.

How do suppliers get listed?

Suppliers create an account, set up a profile with photos and key details, and submit it for review. Once approved by an admin, they appear in search results and can start receiving enquiries from customers.

Can I change my notification preferences?

Yes. After signing in, go to Settings from your dashboard and toggle email notifications on or off. You can change this at any time.

Who do I contact if something isn't working?

If you think something is broken or not working as expected, please use the contact page on this site and send us a short description of the issue. We'll review it and get back to you as soon as we can.
