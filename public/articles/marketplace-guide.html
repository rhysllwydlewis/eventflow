Complete Guide for Buyers & Sellers

Learn how to buy and sell event items safely on the EventFlow Marketplace

📚 12 min read • Updated {{LEGAL_LAST_UPDATED}}

✍️ EventFlow Team 📅 Published: 5 February 2026 🔄 Updated: 1 March 2026

🛒 What is the EventFlow Marketplace?

The EventFlow Marketplace is a peer-to-peer platform where event planners can buy and sell physical event-related items. Whether you're looking for budget-friendly decorations, selling items from a past event, or finding unique pieces for your celebration, the marketplace connects buyers and sellers in the event planning community.

Key Point: The marketplace is for physical items only (decorations, furniture, equipment, supplies). For event services like catering or photography, use our Supplier Directory.

What You Can Find:

  • Wedding decorations (centerpieces, signage, backdrops)
  • Event furniture (chairs, tables, lounges)
  • Lighting and sound equipment
  • Party supplies and tableware
  • Ceremony items (arches, aisle runners)
  • Event technology (photo booths, projectors)

🎯 For Sellers: How to List Items

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Visit EventFlow Sign Up
  2. Provide your name, email, and create a secure password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile with location information

Step 2: Navigate to Marketplace

  1. Log into your EventFlow account
  2. Click "Marketplace" in the navigation menu
  3. Click "My Listings" or "Create Listing"

Step 3: Create Your Listing

Item Title: Be clear and descriptive

  • ✅ Good: "Vintage Gold Candelabras - Set of 10 (70cm tall)"
  • ❌ Bad: "Pretty candles"

Description: Include all relevant details

  • Item condition (new, like new, good, fair)
  • Dimensions and measurements
  • Material and color
  • Any defects or wear
  • Original purchase price (optional but helpful)
  • How many times used

Photos: Upload 3-6 clear images

  • Use natural lighting
  • Show multiple angles
  • Include close-ups of any defects
  • Show scale (place next to common object)
  • Use your own photos (not stock images)

Pricing: Set a fair price

  • Research similar items on the marketplace
  • Consider condition, age, and original price
  • Be open to reasonable offers
  • Remember: buyers want a good deal

Location & Collection:

  • Specify your general location (city/town)
  • Indicate if delivery is available (and cost)
  • Mention if you can meet at public location
  • State your availability for collection

Step 4: Manage Inquiries

  • Respond to buyer messages within 48 hours
  • Answer questions honestly
  • Provide additional photos if requested
  • Be professional and courteous

Step 5: Complete the Sale

  1. Agree on Payment: Discuss and agree on payment method (PayPal, bank transfer, cash)
  2. Arrange Collection: Schedule a safe meeting time and place
  3. Inspection: Allow buyer to inspect item before payment (for cash transactions)
  4. Exchange: Complete payment and hand over item
  5. Update Listing: Mark item as "Sold" in your dashboard

🛍️ For Buyers: How to Purchase Items

Step 1: Browse the Marketplace

  • Use search filters (category, price, location)
  • Sort by newest, price, or distance
  • Save items to your favorites for later
  • Set up alerts for specific items

Step 2: Research the Item

  • Read the full description carefully
  • Examine all photos for condition
  • Check item dimensions and specifications
  • Review seller's location and collection options
  • Look at seller's other listings and history

Step 3: Contact the Seller

Questions to Ask:

  • "Can you confirm the exact dimensions?"
  • "Are there any defects not shown in the photos?"
  • "When is the item available for collection?"
  • "What payment methods do you accept?"
  • "Can you provide additional photos from [specific angle]?"
  • "Is the price negotiable?"

Step 4: Arrange Payment & Collection

Payment Options:

  • PayPal Goods & Services: Best option - buyer protection included
  • Bank Transfer: Quick but less protection
  • Cash on Collection: Safe for local pickups, inspect before paying
  • ❌ Avoid: Wire transfers, cryptocurrency, gift cards

Collection Tips:

  • Arrange to meet in a public, well-lit location
  • Bring someone with you for expensive items
  • Test items if possible (lights, electronics)
  • Bring appropriate vehicle/transport
  • Get a receipt or proof of purchase

Step 5: Inspect the Item

  • Compare item to photos and description
  • Check for any hidden damage
  • Verify dimensions if critical
  • Test functionality (for equipment)
  • Don't feel pressured - walk away if something seems wrong

⚠️ Safety Tips for All Users

Meeting Safely

✅ DO

  • Meet in public places (cafés, shopping centers)
  • Bring a friend or family member
  • Meet during daylight hours
  • Tell someone where you're going
  • Stay in well-lit, populated areas
  • Trust your instincts

❌ DON'T

  • Meet in private homes (first meeting)
  • Go alone for high-value items
  • Share personal financial info
  • Send money before seeing item
  • Feel pressured to complete deal
  • Ignore red flags

Red Flags to Watch For

  • 🚩 Seller/buyer won't meet in person
  • 🚩 Price is significantly below market value
  • 🚩 Pressure to pay immediately
  • 🚩 Won't provide additional photos
  • 🚩 Vague or contradictory descriptions
  • 🚩 Requests unusual payment methods
  • 🚩 Poor communication or delays
  • 🚩 Refuses to allow inspection

Secure Payment Guidelines

For High-Value Items (£100+):

  • Use PayPal Goods & Services (buyer protection)
  • Never use PayPal Friends & Family
  • Document the transaction with photos
  • Get written confirmation of sale

For Lower-Value Items:

  • Cash on collection is acceptable
  • Count cash carefully
  • Provide/request receipt

🚫 Prohibited Items

The following items are NOT allowed on the EventFlow Marketplace:

  • Counterfeit or replica items
  • Stolen goods
  • Dangerous or hazardous materials
  • Weapons, explosives, or controlled substances
  • Services (use supplier listings instead)
  • Items violating intellectual property rights
  • Perishable food items
  • Live animals
  • Items you don't own or have permission to sell

💬 Dispute Resolution

If Something Goes Wrong:

  1. Contact the Other Party: Try to resolve directly first
  2. Document Everything: Save messages, photos, receipts
  3. Report to EventFlow: Email {{ABUSE_EMAIL}} with evidence
  4. Payment Dispute: If used PayPal, open a dispute through PayPal
  5. Legal Action: For serious issues, consider small claims court

What EventFlow Can Do:

  • Remove fraudulent listings
  • Suspend or ban violators
  • Provide user information to authorities (when legally required)

What EventFlow Cannot Do:

  • Process refunds or mediate payments
  • Force completion of transactions
  • Guarantee item quality or authenticity
  • Provide legal representation

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to sell on the marketplace?

Currently, listing items on the EventFlow Marketplace is free. We do not charge selling fees or commissions.

Does EventFlow process payments?

No. Buyers and sellers arrange payment directly using their preferred method (PayPal, bank transfer, cash). EventFlow is not involved in financial transactions.

What if an item doesn't match the description?

Contact the seller first to resolve. If unsuccessful, report to {{ABUSE_EMAIL}}. For items paid via PayPal Goods & Services, you can open a dispute for buyer protection.

Can I ship items to buyers?

Yes, sellers can offer shipping at their discretion. Discuss shipping costs and methods with the buyer. We recommend using tracked, insured shipping for valuable items.

How do I delete a listing?

Go to "My Listings" in your dashboard, find the item, and click "Delete" or "Mark as Sold."

What happens if I change my mind after agreeing to buy?

Be respectful and communicate promptly. Backing out after commitment is poor etiquette but not prohibited. Repeated issues may result in account suspension.

Can I report suspicious activity?

Yes, absolutely. Report suspicious listings, users, or behavior to {{ABUSE_EMAIL}}. Include screenshots and specific details.

Are there any buyer protections?

EventFlow does not provide buyer protection as we don't process payments. Use payment methods with buyer protection (PayPal Goods & Services recommended) and always inspect items before paying.
