For suppliers & venues

Showcase your venue or service to the right customers.

List your venue, catering, entertainment or other event services on EventFlow and reach people who are actively planning an event.

Why join EventFlow?

  • Be discovered by customers searching by location and category.
  • Showcase photos, key details and packages in one clear profile.
  • Receive enquiries straight into your EventFlow inbox.
  • Keep all messages in one place instead of scattered emails.

How it works

  1. Create a free supplier account.
  2. Set up your profile with photos, description and pricing.
  3. Add optional packages to make booking simple.
  4. Respond to enquiries and agree details with the customer.

What you can include in your profile

  • High-quality photos of your venue or service.
  • A clear description of what you offer.
  • Location, capacity and key facilities.
  • Example pricing or packages to set expectations.
  • Any special requirements, licences or restrictions.

Get started

You can create a supplier account in a few minutes. Once your listing is approved, you’ll appear in relevant search results and can start receiving enquiries from customers.
