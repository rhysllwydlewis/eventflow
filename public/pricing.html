Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is always yours. If you cancel, you can export all your enquiries, messages, and analytics. Your profile will revert to the Free plan, so you can continue receiving enquiries at any time.

Can I downgrade or upgrade mid-month?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time and the changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period, and you'll receive a prorated credit.

Do you take a commission on bookings?

No, never. Unlike other platforms that take 10-20% commission, EventFlow charges a simple monthly fee. All booking revenue is 100% yours.

How do lead quality scores work?

Our algorithm analyzes each enquiry based on completeness (budget, guest count, event date), legitimacy (verified contact details, realistic timeline), and engagement (time spent on your profile). Each enquiry is scored as High, Medium, or Low quality to help you prioritize your time.

What's included in the Pro trial?

The Pro trial gives you full access to all Pro features for 3 months at £39/month. After 3 months, it automatically continues at £69/month unless you cancel or downgrade. No long-term commitment required.

How does the business verification work?

Business verification (Featured plan only) requires uploading proof of business registration, insurance, or VAT certificate. Our team reviews within 48 hours, and once approved, you'll receive a "Business Verified" badge on your profile, building trust with customers.

Is there a setup fee or contract?

No setup fees, no contracts. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. We believe in earning your business every month by delivering value, not locking you into long-term agreements.
