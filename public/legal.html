üîê Security Policy

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit
  • Password Security: Bcrypt hashing with salt
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with HTTP-only cookies
  • Database Security: Encrypted at rest, access controls
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • CSRF Protection: Tokens on all form submissions

Your Security Responsibilities

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Don't share your login credentials
  • Log out on shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Keep your contact information current

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability:

  1. Email admin@event-flow.co.uk with "Security Issue"
  2. Describe the vulnerability in detail
  3. Do not publicly disclose before we can address it
  4. We will respond within 48 hours

Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Contain and assess the breach immediately
  • Notify the ICO within 72 hours if required
  • Inform affected users without undue delay
  • Document the incident
  • Implement measures to prevent recurrence
