üõí Marketplace Terms

The EventFlow Marketplace is a peer-to-peer platform for buying and selling physical event-related items. These terms apply to all marketplace users (buyers and sellers).

Marketplace Overview

  • Physical Items Only: The marketplace is for tangible event items (decorations, furniture, equipment, supplies)
  • No Services: Event services should be booked through supplier listings, not the marketplace
  • Peer-to-Peer: Transactions occur directly between buyers and sellers
  • Platform Role: EventFlow facilitates connections but is not party to transactions
  • UK Focus: Marketplace primarily serves UK-based users

Seller Requirements & Obligations

Accurate Listings:

  • Provide truthful descriptions of item condition, size, and functionality
  • Upload clear, genuine photos showing actual item (not stock photos)
  • Set fair pricing based on item condition and market value
  • Specify accurate location for pickup or delivery
  • Update listings promptly when items are sold or unavailable

Legal Compliance:

  • You own the items you list or have permission to sell them
  • Items must be legal to sell in the UK
  • You have rights to all photos and content you upload
  • You comply with consumer protection and safety regulations
  • You handle any applicable VAT or tax obligations

Communication & Transaction:

  • Respond to buyer inquiries within 48 hours
  • Arrange secure payment methods (PayPal, bank transfer, cash on collection)
  • Arrange pickup/delivery logistics with buyer
  • Provide accurate availability for collection times
  • Handle transactions professionally and courteously

Buyer Guidelines & Responsibilities

Before Purchase:

  • Review item description, photos, and condition carefully
  • Ask questions about condition, measurements, or functionality
  • Verify seller location and collection/delivery arrangements
  • Agree on payment method and amount before committing
  • Check seller ratings and reviews if available

Payment & Collection:

  • Use secure payment methods (avoid cash payments for high-value items)
  • Inspect items in person before payment when collecting
  • Arrange safe collection times and public meeting places
  • Get proof of purchase/receipt for expensive items
  • Contact seller promptly if items don't match description

Prohibited Items & Conduct

Prohibited Items:

  • Counterfeit, replica, or knock-off items
  • Stolen or illegally obtained goods
  • Dangerous or hazardous materials
  • Restricted items (weapons, explosives, controlled substances)
  • Services (use supplier listings instead)
  • Items violating intellectual property rights
  • Perishable food items (safety concerns)

Prohibited Conduct:

  • Fake or misleading listings
  • Bait-and-switch tactics
  • Harassment or threatening behavior
  • Price manipulation or artificial scarcity
  • Attempting to circumvent platform policies
  • Identity theft or impersonation

Platform Role & Liability Disclaimer

EventFlow's Role:

  • We provide a platform for users to connect and transact
  • We do not verify item conditions or inspect listings
  • We are not party to buyer-seller transactions
  • We do not process payments or handle money transfers
  • We do not arrange delivery or logistics

Liability Disclaimer:

  • Not Responsible for Items: EventFlow is not responsible for item quality, condition, authenticity, or safety
  • Not Responsible for Transactions: We are not liable for payment disputes, delivery issues, or transaction failures
  • Not Responsible for Users: We do not guarantee user identity, reliability, or trustworthiness
  • No Warranties: Items are sold "as is" between users; EventFlow makes no warranties
  • User Responsibility: Users assume all risks when buying or selling through the marketplace

Dispute Resolution

If disputes arise between buyers and sellers:

  1. Direct Resolution: Try to resolve the issue directly with the other party
  2. Platform Mediation: Contact {{ABUSE_EMAIL}} if direct resolution fails
  3. Evidence Required: Provide photos, messages, and transaction proof
  4. Platform Action: We may remove listings, suspend users, or ban violators
  5. Legal Action: Serious disputes may require small claims court or consumer rights services

Content Ownership & Licensing

Your Content:

  • You own all photos and descriptions you upload
  • You grant EventFlow a license to display your listings on the platform
  • This license is non-exclusive, worldwide, and royalty-free
  • The license continues while your listing is active
  • You can delete your listing at any time to revoke the license

Photo Requirements:

  • Use clear, well-lit photos showing the actual item
  • Include multiple angles and close-ups of defects/wear
  • Photos must be your own (not stock images or competitors' photos)
  • Remove photos with personal information or location markers
  • Maximum file size: 10MB per photo
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP

Listing Removal Rights

EventFlow reserves the right to remove marketplace listings for:

  • Violations of these marketplace terms
  • Prohibited items or conduct
  • Customer complaints or reports
  • Suspected fraud or misrepresentation
  • Inactivity (listings over 90 days old may be archived)
  • Copyright or trademark infringement
  • Safety concerns

Sellers will be notified of removals when possible, with reasons provided.

Safety Tips

For Buyers:

  • Meet in public places for item collection
  • Bring someone with you for high-value items
  • Inspect items before payment
  • Use secure payment methods with buyer protection
  • Trust your instincts - if it seems too good to be true, it probably is

For Sellers:

  • Meet buyers in public, well-lit areas
  • Have someone with you when meeting buyers
  • Count cash payments carefully before handing over items
  • Keep records of all transactions
  • Don't share personal financial information
