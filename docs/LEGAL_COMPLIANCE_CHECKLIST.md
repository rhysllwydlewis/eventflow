# Legal Compliance Checklist

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive checklist for maintaining legal compliance for the EventFlow platform. Regular reviews ensure the platform continues to meet UK and international legal requirements.

## Quarterly Compliance Review

### Q1 (January-March) - Annual Legal Review

- [ ] Review all legal documents (Terms, Privacy, Legal Hub) for accuracy
- [ ] Update legal document effective dates if changes made
- [ ] Verify copyright year auto-updates ({{CURRENT_YEAR}} placeholder)
- [ ] Check company information is current and accurate
- [ ] Review Consumer Rights Act 2015 compliance
- [ ] Verify GDPR/UK GDPR compliance measures
- [ ] Update international data transfer details if processors change
- [ ] Review marketplace policies for regulatory changes

### Q2 (April-June) - Privacy & Data Protection Review

- [ ] Review data processing agreements with third parties
- [ ] Verify Standard Contractual Clauses are current
- [ ] Check data retention policies and schedule
- [ ] Review and update privacy notice if new features added
- [ ] Audit data security measures
- [ ] Review data breach response procedures
- [ ] Verify GDPR rights request handling process
- [ ] Check ICO registration is current

### Q3 (July-September) - Marketplace & Consumer Rights Review

- [ ] Review marketplace terms and policies
- [ ] Check supplier verification process effectiveness
- [ ] Review dispute resolution procedures
- [ ] Verify Consumer Rights Act compliance
- [ ] Check illegal content reporting mechanism functioning
- [ ] Review age verification enforcement
- [ ] Audit user complaints and resolutions
- [ ] Update FAQ and help documentation

### Q4 (October-December) - Year-End Preparation

- [ ] Prepare for year-end legal document updates
- [ ] Review Digital Services Act (DSA) compliance
- [ ] Check Online Safety Act compliance
- [ ] Verify payment security measures (PCI DSS)
- [ ] Review supplier and marketplace activity for policy violations
- [ ] Prepare annual compliance report
- [ ] Plan legal document updates for new year
- [ ] Schedule year-end legal consultation

## UK Legal Requirements Checklist

### Consumer Rights Act 2015

- [ ] Clear returns policy for trader sales (14-day cooling off)
- [ ] Distinction between trader and private sales explained
- [ ] Consumer rights information prominently displayed
- [ ] Contact information for returns and complaints
- [ ] Citizens Advice contact details provided
- [ ] Refund and return process clearly documented

### GDPR / UK GDPR

- [ ] Privacy notice published and accessible
- [ ] Legal basis for processing documented
- [ ] Data retention schedules defined
- [ ] GDPR rights request process operational
- [ ] Data Protection Officer contact provided ({{ADMIN_EMAIL}})
- [ ] Cookie consent mechanism functional
- [ ] International data transfer safeguards documented
- [ ] Data breach notification procedures in place
- [ ] ICO registration current

### Digital Services Act (DSA)

- [ ] Platform designated as information society service provider
- [ ] Illegal content reporting mechanism operational
- [ ] Response times for illegal content (24-48 hours)
- [ ] Transparency in content moderation decisions
- [ ] Terms of service clearly accessible
- [ ] Complaint and appeal mechanism available
- [ ] Records of moderation actions maintained

### Online Safety Act

- [ ] Age verification measures implemented (18+)
- [ ] Illegal content reporting and removal process
- [ ] Measures to prevent children's access
- [ ] Harmful content moderation
- [ ] User safety features and reporting tools
- [ ] Transparency reports (as applicable)

### Trading Standards

- [ ] Business information clearly displayed
- [ ] Contact details readily accessible
- [ ] No misleading advertising or claims
- [ ] Fair trading practices
- [ ] Prohibited items list maintained and enforced
- [ ] Counterfeit goods reporting mechanism

### Companies Act 2006

- [ ] Company name displayed on website
- [ ] Company registration number (when registered)
- [ ] Registered office address (when registered)
- [ ] Place of registration stated
- [ ] VAT number (if applicable)

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High Priority Risks

- [ ] Underage users - Age verification enforced
- [ ] Counterfeit goods - Reporting and removal process
- [ ] Payment fraud - Guidance and reporting
- [ ] Data breaches - Security measures and response plan
- [ ] Illegal content - Reporting and swift action
- [ ] Consumer rights violations - Clear policies and mediation

### Medium Priority Risks

- [ ] Supplier negligence - Disclaimer and due diligence guidance
- [ ] Marketplace disputes - Clear dispute resolution process
- [ ] Service quality complaints - Review system and reporting
- [ ] Privacy violations - GDPR compliance and monitoring
- [ ] Intellectual property infringement - DMCA process

### Low Priority Risks

- [ ] Website downtime - Uptime monitoring and backups
- [ ] Content moderation delays - Resource allocation
- [ ] User confusion - Clear documentation and support

## Incident Response

### Data Breach

- [ ] Immediate containment procedures
- [ ] ICO notification within 72 hours (if required)
- [ ] User notification without undue delay
- [ ] Incident documentation
- [ ] Post-incident review and improvements

### Illegal Content Discovery

- [ ] Immediate content review
- [ ] Removal if clearly illegal
- [ ] Law enforcement notification (if required)
- [ ] User account suspension/ban
- [ ] Documentation of actions taken

### Serious User Complaint

- [ ] Acknowledge within 24 hours
- [ ] Investigation within 7 days
- [ ] Resolution or explanation within 14 days
- [ ] Escalation path if unresolved
- [ ] Learn from complaint for improvements
